SETTING UP METAMASK ON MOBILE

STEP 1

Simply search MetaMask in your phone’s store and install to begin.

STEP 2

After clicking Install, you’ll be welcomed by MetaMask.

STEP 3

Press Get started to start setting up your wallet. After this point, certain phone screenshots are not allowed by MetaMask for security reasons. If you need photos for reference, it’s fairly similar to the desktop version so you can refer from there

STEP 4

In the next step, you can choose to sync your MetaMask to your desktop extension. You’ll have to open the extension on desktop, proceed to Settings > Advanced, ‘Sync with Mobile,’ and scan your QR code. After this, your desktop extension will be connected to your MetaMask.

STEP 5

If you’re looking to have your own mobile MetaMask wallet, click Create a new wallet.

STEP 6

it will ask you to create a new password and confirm that password. Read and agree to the Terms and Conditions.

STEP 7

Next, you’ll create your 12-word seed phrase. You’ll be prompted to confirm that seed phrase by clicking the words in the correct order. Like the desktop version, store your seed phrase safely. The app will also ask if you want to share your usage data. Choose whichever option you feel comfortable with.

STEP 8

After that, you’ll be prompted to take a tour of the mobile app. It will mention that you can create multiple accounts on your MetaMask by just tapping on the mobile icon. MetaMask will then suggest that you create an account name, mentioning that the name should be memorable and distinct, like CryptoCuddles for example.

STEP 9

After creating your account name, the app will lead you to where you can find your Wallet options, Transaction History, link to share your Public Address, and so forth.

STEP 10

Then, MetaMask will relay you to the search page. The app is neatly laid out. It gives organized suggestions for ways you can use your MetaMask application.
